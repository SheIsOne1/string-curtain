Making Of

Scene 01 — Pre-Production
The Idea

It began with a question no one had written down. We wanted to make something that felt less like a film and more like a memory — the kind that surfaces unbidden, already half-dissolved, already beautiful in its incompleteness.

Scene 02 — Production
The Light

We shot on 16mm. Every frame was a negotiation with available light — dawn on the hillside, a single candle in a shuttered room. The camera didn't observe Bella. It waited for her, the way you wait for someone to forget you are watching.

Scene 03 — Post-Production
The Edit

The first cut was four hours long. We let it breathe for six weeks before touching it again. What remained — what refused to be cut — became the film. Silence turned out to hold more than any line of dialogue could carry.
